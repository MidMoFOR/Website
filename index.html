Welcome to the Mid-Missouri Chapter of the Fellowship of Reconciliation

Our Mission

Mid-MO FOR is a group composed of people from many faiths, and no particular faith -- all coming together to support nonviolence and justice. Offering people of conscience an action response to a morally-impaired U.S. foreign policy.

Join Us

We welcome all people committed to peace and justice to join us in our work. Together, we can make a difference in our community and beyond.

Right now our siblings all over this nation are under attack from the fascists we currently have in office. Not only at the national level, but also here at the state level. It is our responsibility to help protect them. But we need help. We can't defeat this unless we are united. Please consider joining us in this fight.

Organize

We strengthen the organizing capacity of often-marginalized communities, with an emphasis on gender and racial justice

Learn

We host teach-ins, community nights, and other learning experiences to strengthen the knowledge of our members and communities at large.

Grow

We convene culture-makers to shift the narrative and use culture as a vehicle for social change.
